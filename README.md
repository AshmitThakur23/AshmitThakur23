<br/>

**Hey, I'm Ashmit!** 👋 I'm a **GenAI student** and **AI engineer in the making** from **India 🇮🇳**.

I don't just study AI — **I build with it.** Every week, I pick up something new — a model, a framework, a wild idea — and turn it into a **working project**. From voice assistants powered by Gemini to restaurant finders that know what I'm craving, I believe the best way to learn is to **ship real things**.

**My vision is simple:** Take the most powerful AI research happening right now and turn it into **tools that actually help people.** Clean code. Clear thinking. Real impact.

📊 **MLOps** — Experiment tracking, model versioning, and production-ready pipelines  
🔐 **Responsible AI** — Building systems that are safe, fair, and transparent

---

**`🚀 WHAT DRIVES ME`**

- 🇮🇳 **Proud to build from India** — proving that world-class AI talent comes from here
- 🧪 **Learn by doing** — I don't just read papers, I reproduce them and build on top

- 🌍 **Impact over hype** — I care about AI that solves real problems for real people
- 📚 **Always learning** — new tech every week, mini-projects, hands-on experiments
